Xenos Missions Division

Mission and Vision

The missions division exists to advance cross-cultural indigenous church-planting movements.  Our long term vision is to see a church accessible to everyone in the people group resulting in changed lives and transformed communities. 

Departments

World Ministries

Focuses on unreached people groups in other countries.  Currently we have accredited teams serving in Cambodia, Eastern Europe, Southeast Asia, Ethiopia and Taiwan.

Humanitarian Aid and Development Fund

We established the Humanitarian Aid and Development Fund to enable people to financially support relief ministries, and be confident that their financial gift will meet real physical and spiritual needs.

Mobilization

This department seeks to increase missions awareness and involvement in the lives of individuals and the local church.

Domestic Ministries

The Domestic Missions department of Xenos Christian Fellowship exists to provide an effective witness for Christ locally.  Ministries include Urban Concern, domestic church planters, Xenos free clinics and a partnership with Central Ohio Youth for Christ.
