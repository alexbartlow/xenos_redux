C&C Reaction Sheets

C&C facilitators usually compose "Reaction Sheets" on their subjects. A reaction sheet is way to focus attention on the subject of the evening and provoke conversation. They usually supply statements from authorities or imaginary speakers that evoke reactions from most people. They also pose questions on issues of opinion. We have several sample reaction sheets you can view and download as well as the accompanying "quote sheets" or "background sheets" the facilitator will either use to read from or pass out along with the reaction sheet.
