Our Curriculum

We provide a curriculum that is designed to teach children scripture while they are still young. We want to include all major doctrines of the Bible, with accurate application and a grace focus. We desire that our kids learn the full council of God and why it is important for their lives.

 

To this end, we have labored to provide age-appropriate lessons that are simultaneously content filled.

 

The curriculum below is available for free download three weeks at a time. An entire year of curriculum

is available for a nominal fee.
