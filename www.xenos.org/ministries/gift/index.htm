Generous Investment
in Future Treasures

Through individual counseling, classes and seminars, GIFT's goal is to deepen the Christian's understanding of Biblical and financial principles on a practical basis. We assist individuals in estate planning, implementing a budget, maximize giving to God's work, and saving for future needs and debt management. Do you need help in establishing a budget or making financial plans? The GIFT Ministry can help!
