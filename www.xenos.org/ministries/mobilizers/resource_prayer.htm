30-Day Hindu Prayer Focus
Join Christians around the world who will pray through the latest 30-Day Hindu Prayer Guide. (Many churches, prayer groups and individuals use these booklets at different times of the year... especially if the suggested dates don't fit in with their schedules.) AVAILABLE MATERIALS:

  • Hindu Prayer Guide booklet (daily illustrated readings 48 pages; 1999 edition becomes available at the end of April)
  • The Gods of India video (1997, 12 min); general introduction overview on Hinduism
  • Kumbh Mela, The Heart of Hinduism video (April 1998, appr. 12 min); covers the largest triannual religious—Hindu—festival in the world; another excellent, colorful, informative and motivating video by Procla-Media productions
  • An Introduction to India and Hinduism six-part video series by Dr. Raju Abraham (1999, 20 min each).

30 Days Muslim Prayer Focus
Calling Christians everywhere to pray for, learn about, and reach out to the Muslim peoples of the world, so that they may come to know God's grace revealed in Jesus Christ. A 10/40 Window project (participated in by Christians worldwide) that coincides with the annual Muslim fasting month of Ramadan. Over a quarter million Prayer Guide booklets were in use around the world during the last Ramadan period. This year the material will have been distributed in about 27 languages, and five to ten million people are expected to participate. Available materials:

  • Prayer Guide booklets (for adults and children), videos (for adults and children), full-color posters
  • Order multiple copies (bulk discounts) for your church, prayer group, ministry, conference, class, mailing list.... Please help and promote this to your constituency!
  • Also available from the North American distribution point: Spanish Language materials for this Prayer Focus!

To receive a flyer with further information and an order form for the next Ramadan period send your name and snail-mail address to: wcn@xc.org, or write to: 30 Days Muslim Prayer Focus, PO Box 26479, Colorado Springs, CO 80936, USA. Tel: 719-380-0507.

Global Prayer Digest
The Global Prayer Digest (GPD) is a powerful daily prayer guide providing vital information to fuel your prayers on behalf of the unreached peoples of the world. The GPD chronicles real-life stories and exciting breakthroughs among people groups where the gospel is being introduced. Daily Scripture readings are combined with geographic and cultural insights to make this a valuable tool that will stimulate you, your family and your church to pray for unreached peoples the world over.
