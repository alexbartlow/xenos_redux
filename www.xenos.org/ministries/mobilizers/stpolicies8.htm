Short-term Missions Step-By-Step
What do you need to know as a Short-term Missions candidate. We will try to explain what you, as a candidate need to know and be responsible for.

  1. Read the Xenos Policy and Procedure packet to become informed about Xenos-sponsored Short-term Mission programs
  2. Decide which trip you are interested in
  3. Fill out and submit Candidate Application Xenos Missions Mobilizers
  4. Enroll in Short-term Missions Class if you have not previously taken one
