Central Teachings

Do you enjoy discussing spritual things? Are you interested in learning more about the Bible? We have a meeting every week that you may find intriguing and enlightening. Hundreds of college students come together to talk about the Bible and interact with one another's spiritual views. Whether you consider yourself a Christian or not, this meeting is a great place to consider questions of God. Our time together includes an expository Bible teaching followed by comments and questions.

Every Monday & Thursday at 8 pm at both the 4th Street Study Center & Coffee Bar and the Pavilion Auditorium
1934 N. 4th Street, Columbus, OH 43201
Free parking

Dennis McCallum, Conrad Hilario, James Rochford and Scott Risley teach these meetings, rotating on a quarterly basis.
