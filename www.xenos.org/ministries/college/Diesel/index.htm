Diesel home church is a place people come to    
  Learn the Bible
  Make close friends
  Join in a team to plant home churches
  Be discipled by mature mentors
Live together in community
 
 
 
Our home church is mainly composed of people who are serious about their spiritual growth. We like to have fun while growing in grace. Retreats, road trips, and warm times of fellowship after meetings are typical. We are always sensitive and welcoming to the many non Christians that visit our group. We believe God's will is to win over hundreds of students to faith in Christ, and that's why we've planted six other home churches in the past seven years.
