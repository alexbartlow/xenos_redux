EDUCATION

B.S. in Pharmacy (1982) Trinity College, Dublin, Ireland

Ph.D. in Medicinal Chemistry [Natural Products] (1988) College of Pharmacy, The Ohio State University, Columbus, Ohio

M.A. in Theology, focusing on Medical Ethics (1994) Ashland Theological Seminary, Ashland, Ohio

RESEARCH in PROGRESS

Along with a group of British scholars, I'm writing a critique of the theory, practice, and ethics of Therapeutic Touch which we hope will be published in a nursing journal.

I'm working on a book on alternative medicine along with David Stevens, MD, executive director of the Christian Medical and Dental Society. We will address theological and scientific issues that arise in evaluating alternative therapies.
