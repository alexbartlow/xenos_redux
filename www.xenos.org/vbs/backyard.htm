Back Yard Bible Club

 

Do you want to share the Bible and your love of God with the children and families in your neighborhood?  Grab a neighbor, find some space, and host your own Back Yard Bible Club!

 

In the past, we’ve had one large VBS at our Main Campus, but we’re moving it into the neighborhoods. Hosting a Back Yard Bible Club is a delightful way to serve your neighbors, meet new ones, teach children, and share God’s love.

 

Designed to be flexible:

  • Co-host with neighbors or folks in your homechurch
  • Run a club in your neighborhood or at a local park
  • Invite as many or as few children as you like
  • Host when it works for your schedule

We'll supply:

  • 5 day curriculum complete with craft and game ideas
  • A template for invitations
  • “How to host” information
  • An e-group for networking with others also hosting BYBCs across the city  
  • A “Summer’s End Club Rally” for all BYBC participants at the Xenos Main Campus  
  

To sign up and receive your materials, please email bybc@xenos.org.
