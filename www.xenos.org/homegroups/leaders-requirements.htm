Requirements for Home Group Leaders

Here's a dilemma: If leadership requirements are too stringent, then few are interested in leading.  But if requirements are too lax, leaders often fail when their groups eventually become unhealthy, causing unnecessary ill will and discouragement.

We have settled on the following requirements for home group leaders in Xenos:

  • Our small group leaders are considered deacons. You can read how we understand the requirements for deacons in the Qualifications for Deacons Paper.
  • We expect all of our leaders to belong to the Xenos Servant Team.
  • Our Servant Covenant is an agreement between all leadership at Xenos and God. It covers basic standards we expect in the lives of home group leaders.
