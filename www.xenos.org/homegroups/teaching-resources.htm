Teaching Resources for Home Groups Leaders

Most leaders don't realize the vast resources available on the Xenos Web Site. There are several approaches you could take to finding what you need.

We recommend starting with a search of the site. Take some time and look the web site over.

Excellent teaching resources include:

  • Hear hundreds of teachings with outlines online.
  • Gary DeLashmutt's Central Teaching outlines include both expository and topical teachings.
  • Christian Principles Class lecture notes are available. These notes are updated each year as the class is taught.
  • Some great essays are available on a wide range of topics.
  • Review the index called Tools for Teaching.
