Walking In Victory

Experiencing the Power of Your Identity in Christ

By Dennis McCallum

Navpress Edition (1994) 1999 Edition CBS Press

Purchase a Copy

A true, grace-oriented study of Romans 5-8.

Walking In Victory is an extremely readable book that brings within reach of everyone one of the deepest meat-truth passages on spiritual growth in the New Testament--Romans chapters 5-8! Thousands of readers are reporting exciting liberation in their lives after reading this remarkable book.

Things you will learn:

  • How and why "Doing arises out of being"
  • Why our sense of identity can only be based on God's view of us in Christ
  • How Paul can say we are "released from the law" without being antinomian
  • How your prayer life can go from dead formalism to living relationship
  • Why "setting our minds on the things of the Spirit" is not referring to a feeling state
  • How we can be concerned about obeying God without falling under legalism
