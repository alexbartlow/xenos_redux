The Summons (Navpress)

By Dennis McCallum

Winner: Campus Life, "Book of the Year Contest" Award of Merit

Realism, Spiritual Struggle and Passion

Now Free!!! (online version only)

 Navpress recently discontinued its fiction line. The result? You now have the opportunity to read this award-winning novel for free through electronic publishing! Tens of thousands bought The Summons for $11.00 per copy.

Download it now in Word v. 6.0 (zipped file=311K) or ASCII (zipped file=289K) and read it on your local computer. NO CHARGE!

The Summons is a deeply emotional story of spiritual discovery, survival, triumph and defeat on a modern secular university campus. Danger can come in many forms, some harder to recognize than others. So can love. Sherry's academic brilliance can't save her any more than her good looks. But even when she meets Christ she finds her struggle has only begun. Instead of "arriving" at a life of self-satisfaction, she finds she has been summoned by God to serve. She and the new pioneering group of Christians on Campus discover how to reach others for Christ, but the Evil One isn't going to let them off that easy.

The Summons delivers deep biblical content while telling a story that will warm your heart.

The Summons will challenge everything you believe about the church and Christian living.
