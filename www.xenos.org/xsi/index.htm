The Xenos Summer Institute | July 9-11, 2014

Practical, affordable training for pastors, home group leaders and Christian workers

What drew 3,300 people from all over the United States to the 2013 Summer Institute? Outstanding content from speakers who know what they are talking about!


"Words fail me to express the blessing, encouragement and sound training you have brought to those of us who have come for the Xenos Summer Institute."

What’s in it for you?

Whether you're a pastor or a Christian worker, you'll benefit from this practical, motivating and affordable gathering.

Practical

Practitioners from diverse ministries with years of experience share innovative ideas and methods in our breakout sessions.  

Affordable

The conference is a proven, worthwhile investment, designed to work within most church's budgets. A group of five can attend for only $250 - that's $250 total, not per person. Our "home stay" program enables you to stay in the homes of Xenos staff and lay workers at no charge.

Motivating

Our speakers will sharpen your vision for ministry in the local church. Be sure to take advantage of fellowship opportunities where you can explore new ideas with like-minded Christian workers.
