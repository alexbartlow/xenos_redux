Christian Principles Class

Find out why this is the one of the most read parts of the Xenos site:

We provide teaching materials for a year-long theology course.
We supply outlines for student note taking in PDF format.
Materials can be adapted to a variety of Bible study situations.

This class plays a central role in the vision of Xenos. According to our Vision Statement, we pursue the goal of reaching the lost, primarily through the efforts of our highly trained team of Christian servants. Christian Principles class is the first prerequisite for consideration as an ordained deacon (a member of the Servant Team).

We offer sections on Wednesday evening and Friday morning. Students are required to complete homework assignments and take quizzes and a final exam to gain credit.

Unit Syllabi

Unit 1: Foundations of Spiritual Growth

Unit 2: Dynamics of Spiritual Growth

Unit 3: Provision for Spiritual Growth

Unit 4: Challenges of Spiritual Growth
