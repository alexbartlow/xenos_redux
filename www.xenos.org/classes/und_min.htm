Understanding Ministry

A Study Guide for Adults

By Dennis McCallum and Gary DeLashmutt

Description

Understanding Ministry is a teaching tool designed to enable groups to delve into ecclesiology and applied theology in the area of ministry within the local church. Most of the Teacher's Guide is also included here for browsing or downloading.
