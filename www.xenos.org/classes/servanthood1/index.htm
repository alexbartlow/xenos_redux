Servanthood 1
A Class for those Seeking Effective
Servanthood in Xenos Fellowship

Introduction to Christian Servanthood 1 is the first half of our specialized training for would-be servant team members. Students must be discipling individuals in their home group and must have completed the Christian Principles series.
