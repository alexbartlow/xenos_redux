Old Testament Theology

Jim Leffel, Xenos Christian Fellowship

Old Testament Theology is a five week class focusing on four crucial themes: how we can know God through the Scriptures; the thematic unity of the Old Testament; the uniqueness and grace of God; and God’s purpose in history—the nature and meaning of salvation.
