Introduction:

Building an effective leadership approach in this area.

1. Review the theology of church and personal finance.



2. Luke 16:9-13 principle ( "He who is faithful in a very little thing is faithful also in much...")


3. Weak giving may be a cause as well as a sign of selflessness. (Luke 12:34 "For where your treasure is, there will your heart be also.)



4. Weak giving often correlates with weakness in ministry.



5. Faithful giving required of new Servant Team members.


6. Review these truths at least annually with your group.


7. Invite missionaries to visit

 

8. Use strong giving members as positive examples-


9. Make giving a topic of discussion in discipleship relationships.
