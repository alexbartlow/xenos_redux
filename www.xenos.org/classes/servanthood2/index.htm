Servanthood 2


Class Syllabus

Introduction to Christian Servanthood 2 is the second half of our specialized training for would-be servant team members. Students must be discipling individuals in their home group and must have completed the Christian Principles series.

Instructors: Gary DeLashmutt , Dave Glover, Doug Patch, Tracy Glover, John Cleary

Heb. 13:17*		   Notebook check next week. Read summaries of three secular counseling models. Evaluate the three models in light of the biblical perspective of the nature of man. Write two positive contributions and two criticisms of each model in light of the biblical view of man.
