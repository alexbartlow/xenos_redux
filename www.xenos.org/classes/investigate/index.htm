Is It for Me?

Investigating Christianity Teaching and Discussion

Isn't it arrogant to say there is only one way to God? Doesn't science disprove the Bible's account of creation? How can a loving God allow so much suffering in the world? Is the Bible really trustworthy?

These are great questions, and ones that deserve answers. Placing faith in God should not require us to set our brains on a shelf and have "blind faith."

If you are investigating spirituality, including the Christian faith, but have questions or reservations that need answers—you should find these five discussions very interesting and thought-provoking.
