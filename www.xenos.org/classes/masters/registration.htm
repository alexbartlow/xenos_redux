How to Register

If you have taken a class at an Extension site before or you have already been accepted into a Trinity program, then please register through FirstClass.

If you have forgotten your user ID or password, contact the Extension Coordinator at (614) 823-6510 ext. 1226.

If this is your first class at an Extension site and you have not applied to and been accepted into a Trinity program, then there is a two-step process for registration.

This is required for both students who are auditing or taking the class for seminary credit.

  1. Please fill out the Visiting Student Admissions form
  2. Please print out this form and either
    • Fax it to:
      Attn: TEDS Extension Coordinator at (614) 823-6530 or
    • Mail it to:
      Attn:TEDS Extension Coordinator
      Xenos Christian Fellowship
      1340 Community Park Drive, Columbus, OH 43229
    You do not need to pay the $25.00 fee listed on the form, but you do need to send your official transcripts to Trinity if you plan to take the course for seminary credit.
