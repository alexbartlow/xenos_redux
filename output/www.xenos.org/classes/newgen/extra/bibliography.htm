Bibliography

\n\n

MUST READS:

(As always, read\ncritically)\n\n

Damon, William. The Moral Child

\n\n

Campbell, Ross M.D. How to Really Love Your Child & How to Really Love Your Teenager.

\n\nThis is a great starter book for\nparenting and easy to apply. Campbell doesn’t believe in original sin which is\nreflected in his writing. How to Really Love Your Teenager has an\nexcellent chapter on anger.\n\n

Chapman, Gary. The Five Love Languages of Children & The\nFive Love Languages of Teenagers.

\n\n

Smith, Tim. The Relaxed Parent

\n\n

Huggins, Kevin. Parenting Adolescents

\n\nThis book contains excellent parenting\nprinciples. Some of his exegesis of the Bible is questionable.
\n\n\n\n

SECULAR, SEX SPECIFIC RESEARCH WITH EXCELLENT CULTURAL INSIGHT

\n\n

Pipher, Mary. Reviving Ophelia.

\n\nThis book can be depressing but is very\ninsightful for raising girls.\n\n

Kindlon, Dan. Raising Cain

\n\n

This book is insightful for raising boys.

\n\n \n\n

RECOMMENDED READING

\n\n

Damon, William. Greater Expectations: Overcoming the\nCulture of Indulgence in our Homes and Schools

\n\nThe information in this book is\nexcellent but it is a more scholarly read.\n\n

Schaeffer, Edith. What Is A Family? & Hidden Art\n

\n\n

Fleming, Jean. A Mother’s Heart & Finding Focus\nIn a Whirlwind World

\n\nA Mother’s Heart\nhelps mothers with having a vision for their families and Finding Focus in a\nWhirlwind World helps with prioritizing.\n\n

Hendricks, Jeanne. A Mother’s Legacy Bible Study

\n\n

This book is a good study for a\nwomen’s Bible study group or personal study time.

\n\n

Tobias, Cynthia Ulrich. You Can't Make Me : But I\nCan Be Persuaded.

\n\n

This book contains a lot of good\ninformation. Be careful not to label your child.

\n\n

Ezzo, Gary. On Becoming Preteen Wise.

\n\n

Kuykendall, Carol. Learning to Let Go

\n\n

This is a good book for fearful or\ncontrolling parents.

\n\n

McPherson, Miles. Parenting the Wild Child

\n\n

This book is good for parents of\nrebellious children.

\n\n

Murray, Andrew. How to Bring Your Children to Christ

\n\n

This book has some good principles but\nit is culturally outdated.

\n\n

Sanders, Vollie. Train Up A Mom

\n\n

This book discusses different issues a\nmother goes through. This is a good personal study book for a mother.

\n\n

Smalley, Gary The Key To Your Child’s Heart Word Publishing,\n1984.

\n\n

This book will help you to evaluate\nyour communication impact with your children.

\n\n

Swindoll, Charles R. You and Your Child

\n\n

This book focuses on the dynamics of the\nparent and child relationship.
