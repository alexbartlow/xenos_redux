Cut these pairs into flash cards. Then in your small group, take turns reading \n one pair to a volunteer. He or she has a couple of seconds to come up with a \n response (just as you would in a real discussion). Follow the guidelines for \n responses from lecture last week. After the volunteer tries his or her hand, \n let the others suggest alternative possibilities.
