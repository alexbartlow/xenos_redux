Week 3: Psalms of Gratitude[1]

Overview

\n\n

Thanksgiving (todah) is related to praise (yadah\n- the verb meaning, 'to praise') linguistically and conceptually. One scholar suggested\nthat all psalms are either songs of orientation, disorientation or reorientation,[2]  which would make hymns and\nthanksgiving similar in some ways but different in others. Psalms of gratitude focus on\nwhat God has done. Psalms of praise focus attention upon who God is. Of course, they\noverlap. God's actions arise from his nature but such a division has its value for us in\nthis class. * \n\n
