This guide is divided into three parts:

\n \n

There is a teacher's manual for you and a workbook for the members of \n your group. You can either select sections to teach over a short period \n (as little as 4 to 5 weeks) or you can go into depth on all the subjects, \n which will take nearly 20 weeks of study (40 weeks, if you meet biweekly).

\n \n

The subjects are marked Level 1, 2 or 3 depending on how vital we think that \n subject is. If you decide to delete some studies, we suggest you delete the \n level 3 subjects first, and then level 2 and so on. If you have the time, you \n should consider covering all the material.

\n

When preparing to teach, be sure to compare the teacher's manual with \n the workbook to see which part your people have. Feel free to share any \n feedback or problems you have with the material with the authors, Gary \n DeLashmutt, Dennis McCallum and Phil \n Franck.

\n

God bless.
