This class deals with the history of modern Israel beginning with the \n Zionist movement in 1800's Europe through to the current day. We will \n look closely at the interaction between European cultural forces and \n the early Zionists leading up to the Holocaust as well as Israel's struggle \n for sovereignty as it played out in the United Nations. Most importantly, \n the class will examine both sides of the current struggle between Palestine \n and Israel along with the international political ramifications.
