1. LAW AND GRACE

\n 		\n

Comments

\n
\n

Knows how to confess sin and claim grace

\n 		\n \n
\n

Able to set goals under grace, and able to shake off failure and carry \n on.

\n

Knows, articulates, and depends on God’s part in ministry.

\n 		\n \n
\n

2. SANCTIFICATION

\n 		\n

Comments

\n
\n

Knows about identity in Christ and correct relationship of indicatives \n and imperatives.

\n 		\n \n
\n

Knows how to worship God in all ways, and with excitement.

\n 		\n \n
\n

Understands the process of sanctification verses quick fixes.

\n 		\n \n
\n

Knows and practices the means of growth as a lifestyle.

\n 		\n \n
\n

Able to withstand suffering with an attitude of faith.

\n 		\n \n
\n

Obeys God much of the time.

\n 		\n \n
\n

Victory over discrediting sin.

\n 		\n \n
\n

Loyal to God over materialistic goals.

\n 		\n \n
\n

Eternal values system.

\n 		\n \n
\n

Convinced that self-giving love is the key to fulfillment.

\n 		\n \n
\n

Familiar with typical lines of attack on hermeneutics.

\n 		\n \n
\n

Knows how to harmonize and resolve most problem passages.

\n 		\n \n
\n

Understands salvation history.

4. THEOLOGY PROPER

\n 		\n

Comments

\n
\n

Knows how God’s attributes apply to personal trust, ministry, and \n prayer.

\n 		\n \n
\n

Able to articulate and defend the Trinity.

\n 		\n \n
\n

Understands how God’s image is reflected in humans.

\n 		\n \n
\n

Able to correct common misconceptions about God and the Bible.

Comments

\n

Able to share own testimony.

\n

7. ECCLESIOLOGY/MINISTRY/BODY LIFE

\n 		\n

Comments

\n
\n

Active in body life at all needed levels.

\n 		\n \n
\n

Knows and embraces the importance of ministry.

\n 		\n \n
\n

Understands what the church is can can distinguish it from the Old \n Testament covenant.

\n 		\n \n
\n

Understands spiritual gifts, church offices, church discipline, and \n church finance.

\n 		\n \n
\n

Has established a personal ministry within, and perhaps outside, the \n home church.

\n 		\n \n
\n

Is a consistent giver, and belongs to the FST.

\n 		\n \n
\n

Seeks or has an opportunity to practice personal discipleship.

8. RELATIONSHIPS

\n 		\n

Comments

\n
\n

Understands biblical love and is able to maintain lasting friendships.

\n 		\n \n
\n

Able to handle and resolve interpersonal conflict.

\n 		\n \n
\n

Practices sexual self-control.

\n 		\n \n
\n

Has adjusted to marriage and fulfills role as spouse and parent.

\n 		\n \n
\n

Has developed relationship-related character qualities (patience, \n kindness, initiative, honesty, etc.), so that he/she is known as a loving \n person both inside and outside the church.

9. PNEUMATOLOGY/CHRISTOLOGY

\n 		\n

Comments

\n
\n

Knows the ministries of the Spirit and regularly depends on the power \n of the Spirit for living and ministry.

\n 		\n \n
\n

Knows the difference between the role of the H.S. in the OT & \n NT.

\n 		\n \n
\n

Ready to answer aberrant teaching on the Holy Spirit.

\n 		\n \n
\n

Looks for where the Spirit is leading and responds accordingly.

\n 		\n \n
\n

Knows scriptures and can articulate the uniqueness of Christ, His \n dual natures, His deity, His work, and His return.
