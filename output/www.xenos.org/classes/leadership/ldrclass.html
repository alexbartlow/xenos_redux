Assignments

\n\n\n

This is a notebook course. That means you will assemble a notebook as the course\nunfolds, and the quality of that notebook will determine your grade for the course. The\nnotebook will become a tool for your use as a Christian leader, so it is important that it\nbe referenced and neat. It should be a 3-ring loose-leaf notebook, which you can buy from\nme while supplies last. There should be dividers by subject. The writing or typing should\nbe neat and readable, and you should include your reading assignments, your class notes,\nany handouts, or other research. The notebook should become a resource you can turn to for\nideas and direction in future years as you lead. We will examine the notebooks at the\nmidway point in the class and provide you any feedback you need on your work to date. At\nthe end of the class, the notebooks are due, and will be graded like a final exam. Be sure\nyou keep up with the reading schedule, because you will need the reading to participate in\nclass.
