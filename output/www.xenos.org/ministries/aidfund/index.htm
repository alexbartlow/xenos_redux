The Bible places great emphasis on the need for Christians to meet both the physical and spiritual needs of the poor (Galatians 2:10, Titus 3:14). Xenos takes this command of scripture very seriously.

\n

The depth of human need in third-world countries is overwhelming. In places like India and Southeast Asia, just getting enough to eat is a daily challenge.

\n

We established The Humanitarian Aid & Development Fund to enable people to financially support relief ministries, and be confident that their financial gift will meet real physical and spiritual needs. Every penny donated directly benefits the poor, since Xenos absorbs all administrative costs.
