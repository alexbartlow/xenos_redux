Our Mission

\n \n

Oasis exists to provide a fun, biblically based environment during adult central teaching where children can respond to and grow in the truth and love of Jesus.

\n \n

Know, Grow, Go

\n \n

Our goal is to help children Know Jesus Christ personally, to Grow in God's truth & their relationship with Him, and to Go into the world sharing the good news.

\n \n

Times and Locations

\n \n

We offer Infant through 5th grade classes at each adult central teaching. For information on special needs students, click here.

Jeff Risley

\n

Oasis Director

\n
\n \n

The best part about working in Oasis is knowing that our work makes a difference in the lives of hundreds of different children and their families each year.    Oasis staff and volunteers have the amazing opportunity to support, develop and nurture children in their understanding of a God who loves them deeply.  I feel blessed to get a chance to work at something that I find truly fulfilling. 

\n \n

My Background

\n

I became a Christian in 1993, but began an active personal relationship with God in 2004.  As a part of a Xenos college home group, I’ve found many opportunities to love and serve people who are both similar and very different from me.   I think the chances to experience diversity and the practical relationship building I’ve seen will be a key area of strength in the Oasis ministry.  Columbus is a very diverse place, both culturally and economically.  I look forward to helping meet the needs of the ever-changing landscape of child development, and am open to feedback from any involved parties.  Please feel free to shoot me an email or give me call, and thanks for the opportunity to be involved in your kids’ lives!
