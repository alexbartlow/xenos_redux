Divorce Care

\n

DivorceCare is a 13-week discussion/video group covering the different aspects of divorce. This is a time to reflect on reasons for the divorce, and to consider a biblical perspective for building a new life in the Christian community. Sharing about the difficulties of divorce with others who are going through the same thing brings resolution and hope for the future.

GriefShare

\n

The Finishing Well Ministry, in cooperation with the Xenos Pastoral Counseling Department, work together to minister to people going through grief. GriefShare is a 13-week discussion/video group for people going through a time of grief after losing a loved one. People gather to eat together, then have discussion and watch a video. Many spiritual issues come up during grief, and it's helpful to meet with people who are going through the same thing. A workbook covering the video discussions is available and helpful to read.

Never Alone

\n

This ministry is to help substance abusers discover and experience the \n hope for change that God offers. Our mission is to help people gain a \n biblical footing for recovery through Bible study, prayer, sharing experiences, and building \n relationships with people who can help through the change. We also want \n to help people find long term success through spiritual growth and loving \n others. For information please call (614) 823-6510 x 1723.

Sexual Abuse Workshop(GRACE)

\n

GRACE is a 10-week workshop that helps women resolve the damage of sexual abuse. The process involves honesty, a change of mind, yielding to God and restoring trust in God. We use the Word of God, prayer, and thought-provoking exercises in a safe, compassionate and loving atmosphere of support and fellowship. Contact Barb Hulett (614) 823-6510 x 1727.

Sexual Integrity for Men

\n

A class to assist Christian men who struggle with pornography to reject \n it, and to replace it with growth in Christ-like character, relational \n maturity and productive ministry involvement. Sexual Integrity is a six-week \n group experience intended for Christian men who are willing to do what \n it takes to find their way out of involvement with pornography and into \n an enhanced relationship with Christ. This includes addressing character defects, \n facing the challenges of relational growth, male sexuality and developing \n in self-giving love. It is more than just saying \"no\" \n to porn. Contact the Counseling Department at 823-6500. More information and resources available at briangardner.org.
