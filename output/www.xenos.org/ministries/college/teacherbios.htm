There are two Central Teachings from which to choose. Dennis McCallum and Scott Risley teach these meetings, rotating on a quarterly basis.

\n \n

Dennis McCallum

\n \n

While in college at OSU, Dennis and his friend started a Bible study in their house, wanting a group they felt comfortable inviting friends to. Their little Bible study has now grown into a church of almost 5,000 people, and he is still here teaching the Bible and training leaders of home churches. He currently oversees a group of college and high school home churches. Dennis and his wife Holly lead a campus area home church and have three adult children who all lead college home churches.

\n\t\t\t\t

Feel free to email Dennis with any questions.

\n\t\t

Scott Risley

\n \n

In addition to teaching CT, Scott leads a home church in the campus area with his wife Chris. Like Dennis and Ruth, Scott oversees a sphere of college and high school home churches in Xenos. Scott earned a BS and MS in computer science engineering at OSU, and worked for three years as a consultant developing web applications. He has recently earned an MA from Trinity Evangelical Divinity School. He enjoys having fun with his daughter, Sophia and son, Matt. Scott loves being part of this vibrant community and seeing people's lives impacted by the love of God.

You can contact Scott by sending him an email.
