In ancient times, shortly after Jesus lived, Christians had to operate underground in house churches. We do the same thing. Each home church of 15-50 students meets weekly to talk about God and our lives. When a home church gets large enough, we divide it and plant an additional new home church. We focus on forming genuine friendships in a warm community. If you're curious about God or about Xenos, a home church is a great place to visit!
