LET'S GET THE BIG PICTURE! Jesus Christ is committed to our family's \n development. He has a plan and the power to change family members into \n becoming more loving — whether a spouse, parent or child. Has your \n family experienced this?

\n

He provides the direction for us to lead our family in a culture with \n many influences — good and bad. He provides the power to be IN \n that culture to reach those that need him most, yet not OF that \n culture. When we fail as spouse or parent, he corrects us, re-directs \n us and provides the actual power for change, even in our character when \n our flaws get in the way of truly loving our children or spouse.

\n

The need of EVERY family is for its members to have a personal \n love relationship with Jesus Christ. Therefore, Xenos Christian Fellowship \n is committed to serving the family by calling out the need for a personal \n relationship with Jesus Christ. Consistent with that, we have developed \n ministry that will help Christian families meet those who haven't yet \n experienced a life-changing relationship with God.

\n

Play groups provide a natural context to meet people and be used by God \n to communicate about the greatness of knowing him personally through Jesus \n Christ.
