The mission of the PlayGroup Network is to share the love of Christ with \n others in our neighborhoods through play groups.

\n

A play group is a small gathering of stay-at-home parents and their pre-school \n aged children, who get together on a regular basis.
\n
\n Most play groups meet on weekday mornings and are typically comprised \n of children (pre-school aged and younger) and their stay-at-home parent(s). \n A play group's structure will vary from group to group, depending upon \n the people involved.
\n

\n

\n Regardless of the structure, play group gatherings provide an opportunity \n for parents to share and receive friendship and support, and have a great \n time together! Relationships that may be further developed beyond the \n play group are established at these get-togethers. Ultimately, play groups \n are a great environment for developing and nurturing lasting friendships.

  • What will you do? Have snacks and coffee, enjoyable conversation, \n discuss books on parenting, marriage, or other topics of interest. The \n children also make friends and learn to play with one another.
