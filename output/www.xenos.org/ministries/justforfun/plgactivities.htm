Special Other Activities

\n

It's typical for groups to enjoy special events together as well, often \n with the desire for all the spouses to meet one another. This might include \n dining out while the baby-sitter takes care of the children, camping together, \n swim parties, family cookouts or a book club for parents.

\n \n


\n Incredible Opportunities

\n

Stay-at-home parents can give and receive support in issues relating \n to parenting and marriage.

\n

Great spiritual conversations can occur.

\n

Children see first hand what it means to love others, and that living \n for Christ is not only important, but exciting as well.

\n

Christian women and men will learn how to do evangelism and learn more \n about being used by God to share how their relationship with Him has helped \n their marriage and parenting.

\n

The play group ministry will encourage spiritual growth in believers \n and in the body-life of Christian co-workers.
