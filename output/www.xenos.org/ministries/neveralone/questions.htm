Q: Do I have to be a member of Xenos Christian Fellowship to attend these \n meetings?
\n A: No. It is helpful, however, to be a Christian or to be seeking to learn \n about Christianity.

\n

Q: Is there any cost to me?
\n A: No. We do share in providing food at the Friday night Never Alone meeting.

\n

Q: If I am already in another recovery program, such as Alcoholics Anonymous, Narcotics Anonymous or Al-Anon, may I still attend meetings?
\n A: Absolutely. We encourage our members to participate in other 12-Step \n programs.

\n

Q: Can my spouse attend with me?
\n A: Yes.

\n

Q: I'm not sure if I have a serious drinking or drug problem. May I still \n attend the meetings?
\n A: Yes.
