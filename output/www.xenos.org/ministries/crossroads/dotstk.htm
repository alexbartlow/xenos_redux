  • According to Dr. Fidelibus, \"The loss of self-identity has been \n associated with some of the most unsettling findings in the entire psychology \n research literature.\" Why is this a concern with postmodern-influenced \n therapies?
    • \n
\n
\n

- Postmodern therapies claim that people are purely the product of \n their socially constructed reality. All that they are and think is not \n their own, but was imparted to them by their culture. Therefore, there \n is no concrete \"self.\"
