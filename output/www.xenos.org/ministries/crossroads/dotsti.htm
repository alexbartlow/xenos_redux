  • Are some events or \"facts\" of history more important than \n others? If so, what makes something important?
    • \n
\n
\n

- Frequency of mention by contemporaries or a clear cause and effect \n link between an event and major changes that affect many people. For \n instance, Alexander the Great's campaign into the middle East brought \n down the Persian empire.

- Events or discoveries that are still considered important or influential \n long afterward to many people can be assumed to be important.
