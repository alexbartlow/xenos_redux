\n Nearly a quarter century has passed since the 1974 Lausanne gathering at which \n Dr. Ralph Winter graphically portrayed the spiritual darkness that grips more \n than half the world's population. Ongoing study and consultations during the \n years since then have only strengthened the conviction that these “unreached \n people groups” desperately need a demonstration of the love and truth \n of the Gospel of Christ. So why are churches still struggling to recruit, \n train, send and care effectively for missionaries?

\n

Mobilization \n is the key. We need to learn to “mine for mobilizers” within our \n churches. Effective mobilizers will be those who are knowledgeable, enthusiastic \n and overflowing with God's love for the lost, both locally and around the \n world. Where do we find such mobilizers? How will we recognize them? How can \n we cultivate within them the passion and skills to mobilize others for mission \n involvement?
