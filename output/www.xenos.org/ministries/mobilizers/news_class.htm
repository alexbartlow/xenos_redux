\n By \n Holly McCallum, Xenos \n director of Missions Mobilization

God \n desires for all people to hear the good news of the gospel. He has given \n the incredible privilege of carrying out His Great Commission to the church. \n

\n \n \n \n \n
\n

Myths \n and misconceptions about missions abound in the minds of many Christians. \n We need to ask, \"How can we challenge our thinking about missions?\"

\n

One \n of the best ways to answer this question is just around the corner-the \n five-week Missions Mobilization I class (starting February 21). We'll \n examine who the unreached people groups are, where they live, and what \n Xenos is doing to meet their needs.

\n \n \n \n
\n

\"This \n class gives the \"big picture\" of God's burden for missions. You \n see how God has always wanted the world to know who He is.\"

\n

\n Stephanie Alms

\n
\n

This \n course will expand your understanding of God, challenge your world view \n and inspire you to find your role in the Great Commission-by either going \n or sending.

\n

\n Guest speakers will talk about important topics like \"Understanding Islam\" \n and Xenos missionaries will share about their experiences around the world.

\n \n \n \n \n
\n
\n

\"The \n missions class opened my eyes to the state of the world, opened \n my heart to missions, and gave me a desire to read and learn more.\" \n

Just \n as there exists a God-shaped vacuum in people who don't know Christ, so \n there is a YOU-shaped vacuum in God's global plan. Missions is not just \n for the specialists. Each one of us has gifts, talents and resources God \n can use to make an impact in significant ways.

\n

\n Missions Mobilization I \n
\n Date: Wednesdays, February 21- March 21, 2001. Cost: Only $30 (Home Group \n Missions Reps may attend for FREE).

\n

Here's \n a peek at our exciting and informative agenda:
\n
\n Week 1-Myths about missions.
\n Missions is one option of many. Missionaries destroy culture. Are people \n really lost?
\n
\n Week 2-The biblical basis for missions.
\n Old Testament and New Testament.
\n
\n Week 3-History and current trends in missions.
\n Why focus on unreached people groups? Gen X and missions.
\n
\n Week 4-Sanctifying our world view.
\n Compelling reasons to choose a biblical world view and lifestyle. Rewards \n and results.
\n
\n Week 5-Serving as Mobilizers.
\n How \n can I use my gifts and talents to reach the world for Christ? Prayer: \n Rebelling against the status quo.
