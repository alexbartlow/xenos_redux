Tim McDaniel is one of co-founders and a key leader of the GIFT ministry. In addition to more than \n twenty-five years experience in accounting and management consulting, Tim has emerged as a leading expert in the business valuation field.

\n

McDaniel is one of less \n than 100 people in the country with dual designations from the American Society \n of Appraisers (Accredited Member) and the Institute of Business Appraisers \n (Certified Business Appraiser).

\n

He has been selected to \n teach business valuation courses to other professionals through the Ohio Society \n of Certified Public Accountants, Ohio CLE Institute, and was a national instructor \n for the American Institute of CPA's Business Valuation CEA Series.

\n

In addition, Tim \n has been published in the national publication, 'Valuation Strategies and Tax \n Strategies' on valuation topics.

\n

He has prepared more than \n 300 business valuations over the last several years assisting businesses in \n strategic acquisitions, litigation support, estate and gift planning.

\n

A 1982 graduate of Bowling \n Green State University, McDaniel is an active member of the Ohio Society of \n Certified Public Accountants, the American Institute of Certified Public Accountants, \n the Institute of Business Appraisers, and the American Society of Appraisers. \n

\n

As a co-founder of \n the ministry to assist people with a myriad of financial issues through Xenos, Tim has seen a lot of unique and recurring situations families face - he is a great resource for all things financial. In addition, he has spear-headed work on a deferred giving program, a growing need for planning in our society. He \n has been involved with Xenos Christian Fellowship since 1985, including \n home church leadership and helping others become disciples of Christ.
