True \n Cost of Debt
\n Total Overspending = $10,000.00

It would be almost impossible \n for any of us to live in America without incurring some type of debt at some \n point in our life. There are, however, some helpful considerations to ask \n yourself before you go into debt:

\n

1. Does the purchase make \n sense?

\n

2. Have I checked with \n \"many counselors\" concerning borrowing?

\n

3. Do I have the spiritual \n peace of mind or freedom to enter into this debt?

\n

4. What personal goals \n and values am I meeting with this debt that can be met in no other \n way?

\n

5. Do I have a guaranteed \n way of paying this back?

\n

Each time we go into debt, \n prayerful consideration needs to take place to allow God to have input into \n our decisions.
