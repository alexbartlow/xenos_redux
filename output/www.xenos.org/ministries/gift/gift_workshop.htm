Workshop \n on Personal Finance

\n

Class Format
\n The class is five weeks long and will be in a workshop format. A workbook \n will be provided to all attendees. During the last session, four to five counselors \n from the finance ministry will be available to review budgets and answer questions. \n Guess speakers from the investment and insurance industry will provide materials \n and speak on those topics. Because this is a workshop format space will be \n limited to 30-35 people.

\n

Course Objectives
\n To present Biblical \n truths on personal financial issues
\n To provide assistance in applying the Biblical truths in the area of personal \n finance in the areas of giving, savings for future needs and debt.
\n To provide tools to assist the attendee in setting-up and sticking with a \n budget, liquidate consumer debt and control future consumption.
\n To gain a basic understanding of insurance and investment issues

\n

Requirements
\n Readings from selected chapters in Ron Blue's book, Master Your Money, \n 1991
\n Completion of a budget and debt liquidation schedule
\n Participation in atleast four out of five classes
\n Pay the fee for the class
\n Bring a Bible, sharp pencil, and a BIG eraser

\n

Reference Books and \n Tapes
\n Master Your Money, Ron Blue (Thomas Nelson Publishers)
\n Defeating the Dragons of the World, Steven Eyre
\n Slaves & Materialism, Dennis McCallum (1Timothy 6:1-11), audio tape
\n “Affluenza” video tape
\n “The Crash” video tape, Burkett

\n Eternal perspective
