This Saturday, Giant Garage Sale

\n \n \n
\n \n

“Every year there is a line at the door at 8 a.m. when we begin,” says John Olmstead.  John oversees the Xenos’ annual garage sale that raises support for the Cambodia short-term missions trip. Be sure to stop by this year's giant garage sale on Saturday April 12th, from 8AM - 1PM at Building X.  People can drop off gently used items (no clothes please, we have plenty) the day before from 9AM-7PM. The team of about 30 students, selected from Xenos’ college ministry, will go on the missions trip this summer.  Students help organize and run the event.

Astounding Response to the Gospel in Millersport

\n \n \n
\n \n

by Gwendolyn Herrick
Nicole immediately began spreading the good news of Christ to all her friends back home in Millersport, Ohio. The response was astounding. So many people were responding to Christ's offer of forgiveness that Nicole, along with a small team from her home church, decided to go to them and provide a bible study instead of making people from Millersport drive all the way to Columbus.  The Millersport ministry began in 2010 on The Ohio State University’s campus.  Nicole Hill, then a freshmen at Ohio State, accepted Christ through the prompting of her then roommate, Taylor Tyndall.

Walk-a-Mile in Her Shoes®

\n \n \n
\n \n

Walk a Mile in Her Shoes® is an event designed to help raise awareness about child sex trafficking.  Each year, men, women and their families join Walk a Mile to benefit Gracehaven.  This event is fun and quirky, yet a meaningful opportunity to raise awareness in the community about local children who are sexually exploited.  This year's event is Saturday, May 10th at 9am at the Grace Polaris Church, 8225 Worthington-Galena Road, Westerville. Register online at gracehaven.me/walk-a-mile.

Need Some Help Around the House?

\n \n \n
\n \n

Ever dreamed of hiring someone to do work around the house while at the same time the payment supports missions? Well then you're in luck!  Rent-A-Student is a fundraiser to raise money for the Cambodia Short Term Missions Trip this summer (2014). Cambodia is one of the poorest countries in the world, where team members will be going to teach the Bible, lead Vacation Bible School for children, assist with construction, and serve at Mercy Medical Center.
