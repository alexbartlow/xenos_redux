Evil spirits often seek and obtain influence over people's lives. Authorities on\nspiritual bondage describe different levels of spiritual influence commonly seen. The\nfollowing material summarizes the findings of Occult authority, Kurt Koch.

\n\n

We remind our students that several physiological and psychological conditions can\npresent symptoms similar to demonic influence. The medical possibilities must be\neliminated before concluding a person is spiritually influenced.
