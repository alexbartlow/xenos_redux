This paper is a study in the history of religion. \"Objectification\" is the\nreligious tendency to reduce abstract principles to tangible, visceral objects and\nrituals. This tendency, found in all religious complexes, has also been prominent in\nChristianity, despite explicit prohibitions in the New Testament. The author believes\nobjectification (also known as formalism) remains as one of the greatest stumbling blocks\nto people considering Christianity today. This paper is the basis for the novel, The Summons (Navpress) by the same author.
