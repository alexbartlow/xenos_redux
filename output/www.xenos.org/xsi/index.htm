The Xenos Summer Institute

\n

Practical Affordable Home Church Conference

\n

 

\n

\n

The Xenos Summer Institute provides practical, affordable training for Home Group Pastors, Leaders and Workers. This year's conference will be held July 10 - 12, 2013 and will feature William Lane Craig, Mark Mittelberg and John Lennox The emphasis will be on equipping Christians to share the hope of the Gospel.

\n \n

What’s in it for you?

\n

For the 17th year, scholars and practitioners share their expertise to meet the needs of your church. Whether you’re a pastor or just getting your feet wet as a layworker, you’ll benefit from our practical, motivating and affordable gathering.

\n

Practical

\n

Practitioners from diverse ministries pool their experience in Breakout Sessions. Here’s where you’ll take hold of innovative methods you can apply as soon as you return to your church.

\n

Motivating

\n

Experienced and challenging Main Session speakers will inspire you to succeed in your home group ministry. And be sure to take advantage of the fellowship opportunities where you can explore new ideas with like-minded Christian workers.

\n

Affordable

\n
  • The conference is a proven, worthwhile investment, designed to work within most church’s budgets. For instance, a group of five can attend for only $250 - that’s $250 total, not per person.
    • \n
  • Our “home stay” program enables you to stay in the homes of Xenos staff and lay workers at no charge.
