Xenos is organized by divisions. Our six divisions each have their own \n coordinator on staff, and usually several staff department heads. The \n coordinators meet with the lead elders to form the management team. They \n are in turn responsible, through the lead elders, to the board of elders.

\n

Adult Ministries

\n

Xenos is a national leader in forming and operating home \n groups. We provide scores of home groups under several different models. \n This division oversees both home groups and our Central \n Teachings (CT). Sphere leaders are responsible for each sphere of \n home groups organized by the CT they attend, providing better support \n and a sense of community at CTs.

\n

Equipping Ministries

\n

At Xenos, we are convinced that equipping lay people for ministry is \n the key to healthy growth. Xenos offers extensive classes, \n teachings, conferences \n and a Study Center to \n help teach Christians about their faith and their ministry.

\n

Student Ministries

\n

Energetic and effective, the Xenos student ministries group reaches out \n to children (Oasis) Junior \n High (Blow Out and JAM), \n High School (North Central) \n and College students (Campus \n Bible Study). We also oversee the Xenos \n Christian Schools. For college students living outside Columbus, Ohio, \n we offer Real Life, an \n alternative to the traditional college experience.

\n

Missions

\n

Missions is a high priority in Xenos. We are committed to sharing \n our resources cross-culturally around \n the world, as well as with the poor in our own city. Urban \n Concern is our nationally known effort to share God's love with the \n disadvantaged.

\n

Support Services

\n

Every day at Xenos we pray we will never lose our excitement for sharing \n Christ's love with people who don't know him. This division oversees adult \n outreach ministry teams; the Servant \n Team, the Communications \n Department and (who operates this Web site); and our External \n Relations team, who organizes our conferences \n and provides equipping materials for \n Christians outside Xenos. We also offer various pastoral \n counseling services to assist people.

\n

Operations & Administration

\n

A lot of planning and networking go on behind-the-scenes in a church \n like Xenos. This includes our present building projects, human resources \n and fund-raising. Take advantage of manuals for human relations or our \n innovative fiscal support \n team.
