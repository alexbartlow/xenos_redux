Elder and Teaching Pastor

\n\n \n

My role here these days consists of serving as a home group coach and a frequent CT teacher.  I also teach a theology/practical ministry class each week for people involved in home group ministry, and currently serve as an elder.  I also started a new adult home group with some friends recently, and that has been a lot of fun. 

\n

I got involved in this fellowship while in college.  After earning a master's degree in history from OSU, I jumped into leadership in the college and high school ministries, eventually settling in as director of high school ministries.  My wife and I have three kids.
