I am a husband and father of three kids, serving as\n lead pastor of Xenos Fellowship, which is a house church planting ministry. \n My wife is Holly, and my kids are Jessica, Joe and Bret. We're all involved\n in this wonderful Christian community, growing together as God uses\n us \n to bless others. Holly serves as Director of Missions\n Mobilization, and Jess and her husband, Ryan lead a cluster of home churches\n in the adult ministry.\n Joe and Bret both lead home churches in the College Ministry and in the\n high school ministry.

\n

I love hanging around with the hundreds of excited and \n motivated Christian servants in our group, and sharing the love of God \n with each other and those outside the church.

\n

I've been here since 1970 when I was a sophomore in college.\n I had recently come to faith in Christ and left the party lifestyle\n to \n follow Him. My brother and I, with some friends, started a Bible study\n in our rooming house because we couldn't find a place to bring our\n friends \n where they would feel comfortable. Our little study has now grown into\n a church of almost 5,000 people, made up of nearly 300 house churches in central Ohio. Unlike a lot of house church networks, we have an eldership and large, city-wide meetings too.

\n

I'm still here teaching the Bible\n \n and training leaders of home groups. Cooking and hunting wild mushrooms\n are my main hobbies.

\n

Apart from my role as an elder and member of the management \n team, I serve in our Central Teachings \n and in the College outreach. \n I teach a course on leadership on Wednesday \n evenings.
