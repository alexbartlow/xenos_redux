Hi, I'm Dave Glover. Thanks for visiting my page.

\n\t\t\t

I serve as coordinator of our Missions Division. I'm also a member of the Xenos Management Team and since 2005 have served as Executive Director of Urban Concern. My wife Tracy and I lead an adult home group. We have 2 children, Lindsey and Tyler.

\n\t\t\t

Prior to coming on staff with Xenos Christian Fellowship I worked as a systems engineer for 11 years. As you can imagine, becoming a school superintendent and missions director was quite a career change for my family and me. We are extremely grateful and consider it a privilege to be in full-time Christian work. However, it was not always this way.

\n\t\t\t

After college I pursued my career while putting my relationship with Christ and wife on the back-burner. This lead to a life of frustration and lack of fulfillment. It was particularly bad because, I was a Christian and I knew I was living a compromised life. In 1984 I came to Xenos where we got involved in a home Bible study. Through the Holy Spirit's conviction and the involvement of close friends in the Body of Christ I gave my whole life to Christ. I decided to put Christ first in my life and live according to His plan and priorities. The best decision I've ever made!

\n\t\t\t

I thank God he rescued me from a life of compromise and lack of fulfillment.
