On staff 16 - 24 hours per week

\n

Assigned a number of home groups for the purpose of providing encouragement, \n counsel, equipping, growth planning, communication of church wide priorities \n and any other consulting needs that may arise.

\n

Responsibilities:

\n

Consultations

\n

Meet with at least one home group leader from each home \n group typically on a monthly basis.

\n

Visitations

\n

Visit each home group as needed.

\n

Annual Evaluations

Meet with the entire leadership team once a year \n to review the Annual Evaluation Form that the team has filled out. This \n is usually at least a two hour meeting that takes place of two monthly \n consultations.

\n

Ensures Database Maintenance

Ensure database information is current \n for each group (includes such things as home group demographics, rosters, \n attendance, discipleship, outreach, study group membership, teaching topics). \n The consultant may directly maintain the database for each group or oversee \n administrative support’s database maintenance.

\n

Promotes Vision and Duplication

Help to provide vision to group leaders \n for the growth of the members and the duplication of the group.

\n

Qualifications:

\n

This staff person should have extensive experience with group leadership, \n group dynamics, and group multiplication. Servant Team membership required.
