The church is commissioned to win lost people to Christ and to help them grow. \n Home groups need to grow in both size and depth to be healthy. Nothing \n is more selfish than a group that ignores those who need Christ \n and turn inward. Each member of a home group should grow in Christ \n and eventually realize they have something to contribute to the \n life of the group and not simply receive. Therefore we advocate \n spiritual growth and ministry within all home groups.

\n

Evangelism \n in the Local Church (from the Understanding Ministry study guide) discusses factors involved in facilitating and encouraging evangelism.

\n

The Evangelistic Prayer Ministry of the Church (from the Understanding \n Ministry study guide) covers how groups can enhance \n their outreach by putting first things first.

\n

Christian sanctification \n and ministry are inextricably entwined. Listen to this lecture \n on Romans 12 to enrich your group's appreciation of the relationship \n between serving love (ministry) and the other major means of growth.

\n

Lead Pastor Dennis McCallum provides some excellent insights into home group \n ministry in his paper, 11 Reasons \n Why Most Home Group Ministries Fail.
