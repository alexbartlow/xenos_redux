The Home Group Consultant meets with his Supervisor weekly to report \n on one or more home groups that they are overseeing. This hour-long meeting \n involves praying for particular groups, discussing the overall health \n of the groups, strategizing, and problem solving.

\n

Quarterly, the consultant provides her Supervisor with a written Home \n Group Report for each of the groups assigned to her.

\n

Monthly, the consultant updates the Home Group Planting chart \n for the groups assigned to him that are planning to plant in the next \n 12 months.

\n

Annually, the consultant provides Annual Home Group Evaluation summaries for each of the groups assigned to her to the Pastoral Division \n Coordinator.
