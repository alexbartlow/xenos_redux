Spiritual Confidence

\n
\n

“Confidence and boldness are the outcome of faith and trust.\n Faith takes into the soul what God in His mercy reveals, and believes\n God against all comers. Trust hands over to God what God has given us\n and says, ‘Keep it Lord, and use it, for I cannot.’ Then\n comes holy confidence and assurance of soul which prevents us from ever\n being disturbed under any circumstances whatever, and out of that confidence\n there comes a boldness which enables us to act for the glory of God.\n Faith, when it has conceived, brings forth trust; trust when finished,\n brings forth confidence and boldness.” H.W. Webb-Peploe “Life\n of Privilege”
