McCallum and Lowery have used personal disciplemaking to raise up scores\n of Christian leaders. Those leaders have raised up hundreds. Speaking\n from their 50 years of combined experience in Xenos Christian Fellowship\n (where more than 250 home churches have been planted), the authors explain\n in practical and biblical terms how anyone can raise up disciples.

\n

This book is intensely practical, but with solid biblical foundations\n throughout. The authors cover eight specific areas that make up the bulk\n of any discipling ministry:

\n
    \n
  • Friendship building - Learn how to initiate and\n develop quality spiritual relationships as the foundation for making\n disciples;
    • \n
  • Scripture -. Discover why and how to impart a life-long love\n of the Bible;
    • \n
  • Modeling - Learn why some models are followed and\n some are not;
    • \n
  • Prayer - Study how to help disciples move from prayerlessness\n to zealous prayer in each key area;
    • \n
  • Personal counseling - Learn how to help your friends\n work through their problems;
    • \n
  • Discipline in love - Learn how to carefully work\n with disciples who become unresponsive to God;
    • \n
  • Ministry coaching - Learn how to lead your friends\n from a selfish view of spirituality to eager service of others;
    • \n
  • Releasing - Go through how to foster independence\n and reliability in your disciples.
