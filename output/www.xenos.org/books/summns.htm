The Summons is a deeply emotional story of spiritual\ndiscovery, survival, triumph and defeat on a modern secular university campus. Danger can\ncome in many forms, some harder to recognize than others. So can love. Sherry's academic\nbrilliance can't save her any more than her good looks. But even when she meets Christ she\nfinds her struggle has only begun. Instead of \"arriving\" at a life of\nself-satisfaction, she finds she has been summoned by God to serve. She and the new\npioneering group of Christians on Campus discover how to reach others for Christ, but the\nEvil One isn't going to let them off that easy.
