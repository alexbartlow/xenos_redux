Vacation Bible School: What's it about?

\n

Vacation Bible School is a summer children's program where kids can learn about Jesus and how much God loves them. Each day the kids watch a drama presentation and enjoy singing together. They then go to their classrooms and participate in Bible lessons, crafts and games. It is a week they never forget!
